### EnvVault

This is a simple method to SAFE share your .env file, just upload your .env or type it directly, then put your own password, then you will get your payload, so your friend will use your qr code or your payload and password or share your .vault file to SAFE access .ev file. the index.html file is open source and you can take it.

You can just drop your .vault file or payload in your github repo or anywhere, and just those have password, can access the .env file.
